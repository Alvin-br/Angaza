Angaza was born in 2023 with a singular vision: to uplift. Founded by Liana Achieng' Rusana and Joan Wanjohi, we have turned community service into a high-impact movement for the marginalized.


From heartwarming events to strategic partnerships, our history is written in the smiles of the children we support and the light we bring to dark corners.
